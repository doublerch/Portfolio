MasterPlan

Ryan Chen, Yoochan Shin, Joshua Shou, Evelyn Shi, Allison Chen
Intended for use by Concordia National Honor Society
Tags: Personal | Swift | App

This app was designed to streamline both tutoring and service opportunities. Students would be able to register as either a tutor or tutee in any field including Math, Science, English, Social Sciences, and Chinese, as well as observe upcoming service opportunities and register for them through the app.
